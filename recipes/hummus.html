Hummus

Description:

You will learn how to make hummus that is better then the store.

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Ground Cumin
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. In food processor, put tahini and lemon juice and process for 1 minute.
  2. Add olive oil, garlic, cumin, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Process 30 secs.
  3. Add half chickpeas and process 1 min. Then add rest of chickpeas.
  4. Add water if hummus is too thick for liking.
