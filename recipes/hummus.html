Hummus

Description:

Here is a recipe on a simple hummus dip

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas or Garbonzo beans
  • Tahini
  • Lemon Juice
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Put Tahini and lemon juice in food processor for 1 minute.
  2. Add olive oil, garlic, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Process for 30 seconds.
  3. Add half of garbonzo beans and process for 1 minute. Then add rest of beans.
  4. If hummus is too thick, add 2 or 3 tablespoons of water.
