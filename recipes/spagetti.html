Spaghetti

Description:

You will be shown on how to make the best spaghetti you have ever tasted.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of dried spaghetti
  • 1 lb lean ground meat of your choice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 can of crushed tomates
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pot on medium high heat.
  2. Cook protein until browned. Break into smaller pieces.
  3. Add onions until softened.
  4. Add garlic, tomato pase, oregano, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Pour in water and scrape up food on bottom of pot.
  6. Stir in tomatoes, salt, and black pepper
  7. Bring to low simmer for 25 min. Stir periodcally.
  8. Cook dried noodles accoring do package directions.
  9. Remove sauce from heat. Add cooked noodles to sauce.
  10. Serve.
